About Me

Hi! My name is Kitana Carbajal Juarez, pronouns she/her/ella. I am a first-gen undocumented student. I am a multimedia artist that focuses my art around the many colors of my community and sharing stories of unspoken voices. My interest of themes are discovering more of my latinidad and the way immigration policies affect relationships within communities. Art is my form of healing and advocacy.

Some mediums I have worked with are; drawing, painting, sculpture, and digital art. I am currently the Arte y Cultura Student Intern at the UCI Latinx Resource Center and a fellow of the UndocuArt Collective with the UCI DREAM Center.I am also working with some students on creating an outside collective to continue community building after college
